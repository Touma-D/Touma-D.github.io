About Us

Our research group focuses on smart buildings, energy efficiency, and HVAC&R systems. We are part of Bert S. Turner Department of Construction Mangement at Louisiana State University.

Led by Zhihong Pang, our team includes graduate students, research engineers, and collaborators working on cutting-edge projects such as model predictive control, heat pump systems, and building resilience.
